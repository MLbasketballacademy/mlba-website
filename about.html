About MLBA

Our story, mission, and the people who make it happen.

Our Mission

More than basketball — we're building the next generation of leaders.

Moses Lake Basketball Academy (MLBA) was founded with a simple but powerful goal: to provide Moses Lake youth with a professional, structured basketball development program that goes beyond wins and losses.

We believe every child deserves access to quality coaching, competitive experience, and a team that feels like family. Through disciplined training, high-level coaching, and a community-first approach, MLBA strives to be one of the premier youth basketball programs in the Pacific Northwest.

🎯

Our Vision

To be the most respected youth basketball program in Eastern Washington — known for developing elite athletes and even better people.

📋

Our Values

Discipline. Respect. Teamwork. Accountability. These aren't just words — they're the foundation of everything we do at MLBA.

📍

Our Community

Proudly based in Moses Lake, WA. We partner with local schools and businesses to strengthen the entire community through sport.
