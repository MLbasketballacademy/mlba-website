About MLBA

Our story, our approach, and the coaches who make it happen.

Our Mission

More than wins and losses — we're developing complete athletes and confident young people.

Moses Lake Basketball Academy (MLBA) was founded with a simple but powerful goal: to give every Moses Lake kid access to quality basketball training that builds real skills, real confidence, and a real love of the game. We meet players where they are — beginners, experienced, and everyone in between.

Our structured training curriculum focuses on long-term athlete development — building a strong foundation of fundamentals before layering in competitive play. When a player is ready for team competition, they've earned it through measurable skill growth. That's what makes MLBA different.

🎯

Our Vision

To be the program every Moses Lake family trusts to develop their player — known for real skill growth, great coaching, and athletes who are even better people.

📋

Our Values

Discipline. Respect. Teamwork. Accountability. These aren't just words — they're the foundation of everything we do at MLBA.

📍

Our Community

Proudly based in Moses Lake, WA. We partner with local schools and businesses to strengthen the entire community through sport.
