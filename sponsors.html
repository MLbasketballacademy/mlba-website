Why Sponsor MLBA?

Your support directly impacts the lives of young athletes in our community.

MLBA is a community-driven program. Sponsorships help us keep registration fees affordable, purchase equipment and uniforms, fund training equipment and facilities, and grow the program to serve more Moses Lake youth. In return, your business gets meaningful visibility with hundreds of local families.
