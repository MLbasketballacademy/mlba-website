Welcome to MLBA

A structured basketball training program for Moses Lake youth — building skills, confidence, and love of the game at every level.

Explore Our Programs

What We Do

Developing every player's potential — on and off the court.

🏀

Skill Development

Progressive training in dribbling, shooting, defense, and court awareness — designed to meet each player where they are and help them grow.

🏆

Competitive Opportunities

When players are ready, they can join our competitive teams for game experience and tournaments — earning their spot through skills development.

🤝

Community First

MLBA is rooted in Moses Lake. We partner with local schools, businesses, and families to make quality basketball training accessible to every kid who wants to play.

🌟

Character Building

We develop more than basketball players. Discipline, teamwork, respect, and resilience are values every MLBA athlete carries for life.

Start Your Player's Journey

Every skill level is welcome. Whether your player is picking up a basketball for the first time or preparing for high school competition — we have a place for them.
