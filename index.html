What We Do

Building the next generation of athletes — on and off the court.

🏀

Skill Development

Structured training focused on the fundamentals — dribbling, shooting, defense, and court awareness — at every age level.

🏆

Competitive Teams

From 3rd grade through high school, we field competitive travel teams that compete in regional tournaments across the Pacific Northwest.

🤝

Community First

MLBA is rooted in Moses Lake. We partner with local schools, businesses, and families to grow a stronger basketball community.

🌟

Character Building

We coach more than basketball. Discipline, teamwork, respect, and resilience are values every MLBA athlete carries for life.
